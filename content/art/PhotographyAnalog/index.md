# My Analog Photography Portfolio: Color Images

These color photographs were all developed and scanned by The Darkroom Lab. Here is a list of the cameras and lenses that were used to produce these photographs: (#1) Canon EOS Rebel 2000 and Canon EF 50mm f/1.8 STM; (#2) Olympus [mju:] Zoom 105. Here is a list of the color film stocks that were used to produce these photographs: (#1) Fujifilm Superia 400; (#2) Kodak ColorPlus 200; (#3) Kodak Gold 200; (#4) Kodak Portra 400; (#5) Kodak UltraMax 400.
